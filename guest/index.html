About NASRDA

Our Mission

The National Space Research and Development Agency is dedicated to advancing Nigeria's space technology capabilities, conducting cutting-edge research, and developing solutions for national development. We harness the power of space technology to address challenges in agriculture, climate monitoring, disaster management, and telecommunications.

Our Vision

To be a world-class space agency driving technological innovation and contributing to Nigeria's socio-economic development through space science and technology applications.

NASRDA Research Centres & Laboratories

Explore our 11 state-of-the-art research facilities dedicated to advancing space science and technology in Nigeria.

Space Business & Entrepreneurship Centre (SBEC)

Promotes space technology entrepreneurship and commercialization of research outputs. Supports startups and small enterprises in the space sector.

📍 Abuja

Institute for Disaster Risk Reduction (IDRR)

Focuses on using space technology for disaster management, early warning systems, and risk assessment. Supports emergency response and mitigation strategies.

📍 Lagos

Climate Change & Agricultural Research Lab

Develops satellite-based solutions for climate monitoring, precision agriculture, and crop forecasting. Supports food security initiatives.

📍 Ibadan

Space Technology Applications Centre (STAC)

Applies space technology to practical challenges in environmental monitoring, urban planning, and infrastructure development.

📍 Abuja

Satellite Communications Laboratory

Develops and tests satellite communication systems, ground stations, and network infrastructure for reliable connectivity across Nigeria.

📍 Kano

Geospatial Information Centre (GIC)

Specializes in mapping, geospatial analysis, and cartography using satellite imagery. Provides geographical data for development planning.

📍 Lagos

Satellite Payload Development Lab

Designs and develops satellite instruments and payloads. Conducts research on advanced sensor technologies and miniaturization.

📍 Abuja

Space Weather & Ionospheric Research Centre

Studies space weather phenomena, solar activity, and ionospheric effects on communications and navigation systems.

📍 Calabar

Launch Vehicle Technology Centre

Researches and develops indigenous launch vehicle technologies and propulsion systems for Nigeria's space program.

📍 Enugu

Ground Station Operations Centre

Manages satellite tracking, telemetry, command operations, and data reception from space-based assets. Provides 24/7 mission operations support.

📍 Abuja

Space Science Education & Outreach Centre

Promotes STEM education, conducts public awareness campaigns, and trains the next generation of space scientists and engineers.

📍 Port Harcourt

NASRDA Organizational Departments

Our 10 core departments work together to achieve NASRDA's mission of advancing Nigeria's space capabilities.

Office of the Director-General

Provides strategic leadership, policy guidance, and overall direction for NASRDA. Handles international relations and stakeholder engagement.

Research & Innovation Department

Oversees all research activities, innovation initiatives, and technology development programs. Manages research ethics and intellectual property.

Space Technology Operations

Manages satellite operations, ground stations, mission control, and space asset management. Ensures optimal performance of space systems.

Finance & Administration

Handles budgeting, financial planning, procurement, human resources, and administrative operations for all NASRDA divisions.

Engineering & Technical Services

Provides engineering expertise, technical support, and infrastructure maintenance. Oversees facilities and equipment management.

Legal & Compliance

Ensures regulatory compliance, manages legal affairs, handles contracts, and enforces adherence to national and international space laws.

Public Communications & Outreach

Manages media relations, public information, educational programs, STEM promotion, and agency communications strategy.

International Relations & Partnerships

Develops international collaborations, manages bilateral agreements, coordinates with space agencies, and handles diplomatic engagements.

Information Technology & Cybersecurity

Manages IT infrastructure, data security, cybersecurity protocols, software systems, and digital transformation initiatives.

Quality Assurance & Safety

Ensures quality standards, workplace safety, risk management, and compliance with safety protocols across all NASRDA operations.
